Every product on TAFESSE has been sourced from a verified supplier. What you see is what arrives. No substitutions, no surprises.

How it works

No formal address needed. Drop a Google Maps pin, share GPS coordinates, or add delivery notes. We've built this for Addis — not for a city with neat postcodes.

Choose a day and a window — morning, afternoon or evening. We don't give you a vague "sometime today." We give you a time and we keep it.
