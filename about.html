Our Story

Founded in London. Built for Addis.

TAFESSE was born from a simple belief — that Addis Ababa deserves brands that match its ambition. Reliable, professional, trustworthy brands that people can depend on.

The vision
Addis Ababa is the future capital of Africa. Others are finally seeing what we always saw.

The city is on the rise. New apartments, new businesses, a rising generation with rising expectations. Investment is flowing in. The world is paying attention. Addis Ababa is becoming one of the great cities of this century.

And yet — when it comes to buying something for your home, ordering it reliably, having it arrive on time and assembled properly — that infrastructure does not yet exist. There is no trusted home commerce brand. No reliable delivery. No quality guarantee.

That is the gap TAFESSE is built to fill. Not as a quick business opportunity — but as a long-term commitment to building something Addis can be proud of.

What we stand for
Our values

Everything we do comes back to these four principles.

01
Trust above all

Every decision we make is filtered through one question — does this make us more trustworthy? If the answer is no, we do not do it.

02
Delivery is the product

The sofa is not the product. The fridge is not the product. The experience of ordering it, receiving it, and having it work perfectly — that is the product.

03
Built for Addis

We are not copying a Western model and dropping it into Ethiopia. We are building something designed from the ground up for how Addis actually works.

04
Long term thinking

We are not here for quick wins. We are building a brand that will still be here in 20 years — one that Addis can rely on the way London relies on John Lewis.

The journey
Where we are going
2026
Launch — Addis Ababa

First supplier partnerships confirmed. First deliveries made. Building the trust brand one perfect order at a time. Furniture and appliances, white-glove delivery, Addis-wide.

2027
Growth — The platform expands

More supplier partners. Home setup packages. A dedicated delivery team. The diaspora channel fully open — order from anywhere in the world for family in Addis.

2028

TAFESSE becomes the standard for home commerce in Addis. Third-party sellers operating under the TAFESSE quality guarantee. Fulfilment services for other businesses.

2030+
Expansion — Beyond Addis

The TAFESSE model expands to other Ethiopian cities and beyond. A pan-African premium home commerce brand — built in Addis, recognised everywhere.
